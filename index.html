About Me
“Great developer”

Since I was five, I loved playing board games like go, both Chinese and western chess. Since those games require you to forethink the aspect of the game and predict what your opponent’s next moves are, it is very similar to coding because it also allows you to make your hypothesis into actual results.

 

Like those board games, things are very vague and dim in coding at first. But, you will see later that each logical choice you made will blend to make an art of strategy. And, every new strategy requires continuous improvements to encounter new challenges. An experienced developer can be achieved by just getting more experiences. But, I will rather be a "great developer" who will never stop learning everyday to become a better one.

 

When I am not working, I am an avid video game player, coffee lover, Netflix binger, and movie lover. (Yes, I am good at go and Chinese chess!)
